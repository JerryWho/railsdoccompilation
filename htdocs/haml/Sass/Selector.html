Overview

A namespace for nodes in the parse tree for selectors.

Selector::CommaSequence is the toplevel seelctor, representing a comma-separated sequence of Selector::Sequences, such as foo bar, baz bang. Selector::Sequence is the next level, representing Selector::SimpleSequences separated by combinators (e.g. descendant or child), such as foo bar or foo > bar baz. Selector::SimpleSequence is a sequence of selectors that all apply to a single element, such as foo.bar[attr=val]. Finally, Selector::Simple is the superclass of the simplest selectors, such as .foo or #bar.
